About Me

Greetings and thank you for checking out my personal portfolio page! My name is Nicholas Donato and I am a certified Full Stack Developer from the University of Central Florida's Coding Bootcamp. In addition to obtaining my certificate in Full Stack Development from UCF, I additionally am a 2014 graduate from Penn State University with a BA in Economics, during which time I also took multiple computer science courses to expand my knowledge and critical thinking skills prior to my decision to become a developer. Prior to my recent training, I used my knowledge of C++ and excel VBA to conduct statistical database modeling programs while working for a major brokerage firm on Wall Street in New York City. During this time I also tested my at the time limited python skills to further construct algorithmic data driven modeling programs for the VIX and S&P 500. Since then I have expanded my skills and expertise into multiple areas which include: JavaScript, jQuery, NodeJS, Express(.json), ReactJS, HTML5/CSS3, Bootstrap, Materialize, Git(Version Control), Responsive Design, API/JSON, PHP, RESTful API, Firebase, Heroku, MySQL + Workbench, MongoDB, AJAX, Sequelize SEO, Node Express, CRUD Operations, POSTMAN Angular, AngularJS and Database Modeling, Ruby On Rails among others such as Python, C++, Java, R, C#.

I am a determined Developer still learning every day. Whether it is contstructing interactive websites from scratch or coding applications with API's and/or constructed databases I am versed with the logic and operations that goes into constructing something from scratch or applying changes to an existing program or application. The most valuable aspects of my training has been working together with team members to organize and divy out tasks in seperate parts to complete a final goal with proper communication and set up.
